Site-First Thinking

Every design starts with the land. We study sun angles, prevailing winds, views worth framing, and privacy needs before putting anything on paper.

The team that designed your home is the same team building it. No miscommunication between architect and contractor, because they're the same people.

Built for This Climate

We pick materials and construction methods that work in Lombok's heat, humidity, and salt air. If something won't last here, we don't use it.

Step by Step

How a Project Comes Together

1

First Meeting & Site Visit

We visit the site, understand your brief, discuss budget expectations, and assess any regulatory requirements. This initial step is free and helps both sides decide if we're a good fit.

2

Concept Design

Based on the site study, we develop initial design options: floor plans, massing, material direction. You'll see how the building sits on the land and how the spaces flow before we commit to anything.

3

Design Development & Drawings

Once the concept is approved, we develop detailed construction drawings. Structural plans, material specifications, MEP (mechanical, electrical, plumbing) coordination. Everything the build team needs to start.

4

Construction & Handover

We manage the build on-site with regular progress updates. You'll know exactly where things stand at every stage. When the building is done, we walk through it together before we hand over the keys.

A Studio Built on Local Knowledge

Atelier Nusa is based in Mataram, Lombok. We've been designing and building across the island long enough to know which materials last, which contractors deliver, and how to navigate the local building landscape.

That practical knowledge is what separates a building that looks good in renders from one that actually works, five, ten, twenty years down the line.
