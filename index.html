What We Do

From First Sketch to Final Build

We handle architecture and construction under one roof. No handoffs, no lost in translation moments. Just a clear line from design to finished building.

Architectural Design

Every project starts with the site. We study the land, the sun path, the wind, and the neighbourhood before putting pen to paper. The result is architecture that feels like it belongs where it stands.

Design & Build

When the same team designs and builds, nothing gets lost along the way. We manage the entire process so the building you get is the building we designed, down to the last detail.

Construction Management

We know how things get built in Lombok. From material sourcing to on site coordination, we keep quality tight and timelines realistic. No surprises, no shortcuts.

How We Work

Built for the Tropics

Lombok's climate is hot, humid, and occasionally fierce, whether you're building beachfront in Kuta Mandalika, hillside in Senggigi, or among the rice fields of West Lombok. That's not a problem. It's a brief. We design with deep overhangs, cross ventilation, and materials that can handle salt air and monsoon rain.

What Sets Us Apart

We Design for the Site

No copy paste designs. A beachfront villa in Kuta Mandalika needs a different approach than a family home in Mataram or a hillside retreat in Senggigi. Every project responds to its specific land.

One Team, Start to Finish

Design and construction handled by the same studio. That means fewer misunderstandings, faster decisions, and a building that actually matches the drawing.

Local Knowledge Matters

Based in Mataram and working across Lombok, from the Gili Islands to Kuta, Senggigi to East Lombok. We know the local materials, the reliable suppliers, and how to build things that last.
